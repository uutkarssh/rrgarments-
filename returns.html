Help

RETURNS

We offer easy 7-day returns and exchanges on unworn items.

7-DAY RETURN POLICY

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to request a return or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

HOW TO RETURN

  • Bring the item(s) to our store at Gopiganj Road, between Pillar No. 21 & 22
  • Bring your order confirmation email or order number
  • Our team will inspect the item and process your refund or exchange

REFUNDS

Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item. You'll get an email confirmation once the refund is processed.

EXCHANGES

To exchange an item for a different size or color, visit our store with the original item and receipt. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Undergarments and accessories for hygiene reasons
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
