Everything you need to know about how we ship your orders.

PROCESSING TIME

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday to Saturday, excluding holidays). You'll receive a confirmation email with your order number once your order ships.

LOCAL PICKUP

Visit our store at Gopiganj Road, between Pillar No. 21 & 22 to pick up your order. Select "Local Pickup" at checkout and we'll have it ready within 2 hours.

ORDER TRACKING

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your package through the courier's website.

We currently ship within India only. International shipping is coming soon — follow us on Instagram for updates.
