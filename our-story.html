About

OUR STORY

Born from a love of minimalism and streetwear culture.

HOW IT STARTED

RR Garments began with a simple idea: create high-quality, minimalist clothing that speaks through its simplicity. Founded on Gopiganj Road, we started as a small store with a big vision — to bring streetwear-influenced essentials to our community.

What began as a passion project has grown into a curated collection of monochrome essentials, each piece designed with intention and crafted to last.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We believe in less, but better. Every piece in our collection is designed to be versatile, durable, and timeless. No fast fashion, no fleeting trends — just clean, considered clothing you'll reach for again and again.

Our monochrome palette with a single olive accent isn't just a design choice; it's a statement about restraint and confidence.

MADE RESPONSIBLY

We work with trusted manufacturers who share our commitment to fair labor practices and environmental responsibility. Our fabrics are sourced from suppliers who prioritize organic and recycled materials.

VISIT US

We're more than an online store. Visit us at Gopiganj Road, between Pillar No. 21 & 22 to see and feel our collection in person. Our team is always happy to help you find the perfect piece.
