TERMS & POLICY

How ordering works, returns, privacy, and store policies.

HOW ORDERING WORKS

We follow a simple, phone-confirmed ordering process — no online payment required upfront. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1 — Place Order: You browse the store, add products to your cart, and place the order with your shipping details (name, address, PIN, phone number).
  • Step 2 — Owner Call: After you place the order, the owner personally calls you on your contact number to confirm the order, verify details, and discuss payment method.
  • Step 3 — Order Confirmed: Once the owner confirms over the phone, your order status changes to "Confirmed" and it moves to packing.
  • Step 4 — Pack & Dispatch: Your order is packed and handed to the delivery partner. You can track the status in real-time.
  • Step 5 — Delivery: The order is delivered to your address. You can track every step on our Order Tracking page.

ORDER TRACKING

Every order gets a unique Tracking ID (e.g., RRG1234567890) shown on the confirmation page. Use this ID on our Track Order page to see the current status:

  • Order Placed — You placed the order. Waiting for owner confirmation.
  • Confirmed — Owner called and confirmed the order.
  • Packed — Order is packed and ready for dispatch.
  • Dispatched — Order handed to delivery partner.
  • In Transit — Order is on the way to you.
  • Delivered — Order delivered successfully. 🎉

PAYMENT POLICY

We don't process online payments on the website. Payment is arranged directly with the owner during the confirmation call. Accepted methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • UPI / Google Pay / PhonePe
  • Bank Transfer

The owner will confirm the payment method during the call.

7-DAY RETURN POLICY

You have 7 days from delivery to request a return or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags. Visit our store at Gopiganj Road, between Pillar No. 21 & 22 with the product and order number. See our Returns page for details.

SHIPPING

Free shipping on orders over ₹2,000. Standard shipping (3–5 business days) costs ₹99 below ₹2,000. Local pickup is free from our store. See our Shipping page for full details.

PRIVACY POLICY

We collect your name, address, phone number, and email (optional) solely for processing and delivering your order. Your information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties except for delivery purposes. Your phone number is used only by the owner to confirm your order.

CONTACT

Questions about your order? Contact us or visit our store at Gopiganj Road, between Pillar No. 21 & 22. You can also track your order anytime using your tracking ID.
